## Project Description
My project will be based on a childhood video game I used to play on my ipad. You control a little pixlated bird in a plane as he flies through different levels, fighting
different enemies and unique bosses. The environment of each level is different, with some that I remember being in space, with others being in amuesment parks and desserts.

Needs to have:
- 1 useable character
- 3 different normal enemy types
- 1 boss
- sound effects
- background music
- changing background
- main menu
- 1 playable level
- attack projectiles (enemies and friendly)
- level should be ~ 2 minutes long
 
Nice to have
- more than 1 playable character (2 - 3 would be nice)
- more than 1 level
- 1 or 2 new bosses with the the addition of each level
- 2 - 3 new normal enemy types in every new level
- different background with each new level
- implementing health packs and powerups
- tutorial
